Selective Disclosure Report Ledger

A confidential, append-only bug-report ledger. Existence and ordering are provable to anyone; contents are revealed only by the Operator, field by field. Open each role in its own window and play them side by side.

Member

Governance. Initialise the issuer signing key. Do this first.

Client

Anonymous. File a report and get back a sealed, verifiable token.

Operator

Read reports in full and release partial, field-level disclosures.

Researcher

Verify a released disclosure offline against the endorsed issuer key.

Suggested order: Member initialises the key → Client submits → Operator discloses a field → Researcher verifies.
